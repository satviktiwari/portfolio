Framework for CCTV Storage Optimization

I developed a computer program and a website to optimize the storage space of CCTV cameras using the YOLO-v5 algorithm and OpenCV library. It optimizes the storage space by removing the redundant CCTV footage frames having Euclidean distance lesser than a threshold value. The algorithm efficiently reduces the storage space of the recordings to about one-fourth of the existing space without any compromise on the quality of the recordings.
